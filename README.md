## 3. Featured Project: Aviation Sector Synergy Model

### 3.1 Objective

Calculate the **total synergy potential** for a **target airline with a $100M revenue base**, and estimate the **EBIT uplift** post-integration.

### 3.2 Key Findings

- **Total Synergy Potential**  
  - Identified a **conservative case** of **$9.95M** in synergies.  
  - Identified an **aggressive case** of **$18.3M** in synergies.  

- **Cost Drivers & Synergy Levers**  
  Modeled savings across **five core categories**:  
  - **G&A / Back-office**  
  - **Fuel**  
  - **Aircraft leasing**  
  - **Maintenance**  
  - **Software licensing**  

- **Impact on Profitability**  
  Demonstrated a **meaningful step-up in target profitability** from a base of **5% EBIT** to a **directional range of 14.95%–23.30%** under different synergy realization scenarios.

## 4. Featured Project: Strategic Divestiture & M&A

### 4.1 Microsoft / Activision Gaming Merger Case

- Analyzed the **strategic rationale** for combining:  
  - **Xbox hardware and gaming ecosystem**,  
  - **King’s mobile gaming expertise** (creator of **Candy Crush**, contributing **$2.5B+ in annual mobile gaming revenue** in recent years),  
  - **Azure and AI technology stack** supporting personalization, cloud delivery, and at-scale gaming infrastructure.  

- Highlighted **synergy themes** including:  
  - Expansion into **mobile gaming** and cross-platform experiences.  
  - Monetization through **in-game microtransactions and advertising**.  
  - Leveraging **cloud and AI infrastructure** for improved gaming performance and analytics.  

### 4.2 Wearables Divestiture Framework

- Structured a **CFO-level framework** to evaluate the **strategic divestiture** of a **wearables business unit**, focusing on:  
  - **Operational entanglement**: shared functions, systems, and processes between the parent and the unit.  
  - **Shared IP & licensing risks**: patents, algorithms, UX design, and data-sharing arrangements.  
  - **Transition services** and separation costs.  

- Designed an **18-question interview guide** to systematically probe:  
  - Revenue dependencies and cross-selling.  
  - Cost allocations and shared infrastructure.  
  - IP ownership, licensing, and post-divestiture restrictions.
